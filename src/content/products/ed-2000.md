The ED-2000 is VortKart's versatile electric drift kart, featuring a selectable 2000W–4000W PMSM motor and 72V lead-acid battery. Delivering 60 km/h top speed with 3.3s acceleration to 50 km/h, hydraulic disc brakes, and an LCD dashboard, it's built for drift tracks and high-throughput commercial venues.
