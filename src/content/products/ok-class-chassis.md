The OK Class Chassis is VortKart's competition-grade racing platform, engineered for club and national-level karting. Built with 4130 Cr-Mo steel and 30×2.0mm precision tubing, it accepts all major engine packages including X30, ROK, and ROTAX. Featuring iBox8 hydraulic floating ventilated disc brakes with rear braking for superior stopping power on track.
